GTUGs

GTUGs in the area near you

GDG Tel Aviv

GTUG Tel Aviv is the central hub of GTUGers in Israel. GTUG Tel Aviv hosts a wide range of speakers using many different Google related technologies (Android, Chrome, HTML5, AppEngine, etc.). We also have non-technical lectures every once in a while.

GDG Herzeliya

This group is a joint venture of 4 organizations:

  • GDG Herzeliya - a group that is sponsored and endorsed by Google in order to aid the communities that use their technology (Android, Chrome, HTML5, AppEngine, etc.)
  • The Israeli Android Association - focusing on two main facets, building a community that creates a common ground for networking with Android developers in Israel, and providing knowledge in latest technologies through organizing lectures, events, labs, etc.
  • The Cantina - an NPO that has set out to create and nurture open source groups and communities by organizing events that feature lectures and Hackathons
  • The Media Lab at IDC Herzeliya which hosts most of our events
The group features events that include technical lectures and themed hackathons mostly focused on Android OS.

GDG Jerusalem

The Jerusalem GTUG is the place for developers from around Jerusalem to discuss Google-related technologies, including Android, HTML5, cloud, JavaScript frameworks, and more.

GDG Beer Sheva

GDG Beer Sheva is a place to meet developers from the Negev area to discuss about the Google-related technologies. Come and meet great people, talk about technologies, learn cool new things and all of that in a very fun atmosphere!
